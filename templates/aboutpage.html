Sweet, Smart, and Simple

At Bitly, we know that desserts aren’t just food — they’re joy, comfort, and sometimes a well-deserved reward. But when it comes to managing your health, even sweet treats deserve a little mindfulness. That’s why we created an easy-to-use web app that helps you recognize desserts and estimate their calories with just one photo.

Because yes, you can enjoy your cake — and track it too.

What We Do

We use AI to identify desserts from images and provide calorie estimates based on a standard 100-gram serving size or you can customize the serving size. Our app helps you stay in control without taking the sweetness out of your life.

Why Just Desserts?

Because desserts are often the trickiest to estimate — and the most tempting to skip tracking. We focus on sweets to give dessert-lovers, fitness-watchers, and curious snackers a simple tool built just for them.
